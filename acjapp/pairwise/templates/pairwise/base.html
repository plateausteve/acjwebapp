CDAT Project

The Equitable Recognition of Art Talent Using Clark’s Drawing Abilities Test project compares two ways of recognizing art talent among students of diverse demographics: Teacher observation and holistic assessment of student drawings using this online webapp.

NAEF Research Grant

The CDAT Project is funded by a 2021 grant from the National Art Education Foundation and conducted by Steve Heil, Principal Investigator.
